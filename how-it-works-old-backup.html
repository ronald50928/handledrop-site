Trade in four simple steps

1) List & Value

List your item with photos and set a value band. Bands keep things simple and reduce haggling.

2) Match & Agree

Matches show candidate trades. Both parties approve — consent gates ensure no one ships before agreement.

3) Ship Privately

Masked addresses, locker options, and prepaid labels for Premium. DIY labels on Free.

4) Verify & Rate

On delivery, both verify condition and leave ratings. Trust builds over time.

Security callout

  • Temporal address tokens & masking
  • Consent gates before shipping
  • Optional in‑store ID verification
