Pricing FAQs

Can I start free and upgrade later?

Absolutely! Start with the free tier and upgrade whenever you want. You can also downgrade back to free at any time.

What are "value gap limits"?

On the free tier, if items have different values, you pay a small band fee ($5-10 typically). Premium removes these fees when trading with other Premium members.

How does the free label work?

Premium members get one prepaid shipping label per month (value ~$8). If you don't use it, it doesn't roll over, so make sure to trade monthly!

Can I cancel Premium anytime?

Yes! Cancel anytime, no questions asked. You'll keep Premium benefits until the end of your billing period.

Are there any hidden fees?

Nope! You only pay for membership (if Premium) and shipping. That's it. No listing fees, success fees, or surprise charges.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Coming soon! We're launching an annual plan at $59/year (2 months free). Join our waitlist to get notified.
