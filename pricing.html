FAQ

Why no cash?

HandleDrop never moves money between users. It’s a goods‑for‑goods exchange with simple value bands.

How do value bands work?

Bands reduce haggling. Agree a range, then ship when both approve. If values diverge, adjust or decline.

Do both need Premium for unequal trades?

Yes. Unlimited gap is enabled when both participants are Premium.
