Common Questions

What if I don't like what I receive?

Both parties confirm the trade before it's finalized. If something isn't as described, you can dispute within 48 hours. We'll help resolve it or reverse the trade.

How do you determine fair value?

Our AI analyzes millions of completed trades, current market prices, condition, and demand. You always see suggested value ranges before committing.

What can't I trade?

No weapons, hazardous materials, living things, or illegal items. We also restrict items that require special licensing (alcohol, tobacco, regulated medications).

Is my address really private?

Yes! We use proxy addresses. The other person never sees your real address, and you never see theirs. Maximum privacy.

What if the other person backs out?

No problem. Your item ships back to you at no cost if someone cancels after shipping has started.

How long does a typical trade take?

Most trades complete in 5-7 days from match to delivery. Fast local trades can be done in 2-3 days.
